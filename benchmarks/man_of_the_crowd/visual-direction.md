# Visual Direction: The Man of the Crowd

## Approved Benchmark Scene

The narrator observes a gaslit London crowd through the D—— Coffee-House bow window, sees the old man's singular face, leaves the coffeehouse, and glimpses a diamond and dagger beneath the stranger's torn roquelaire.

## Character Locks

### Narrator

- Adult man recovering from illness; pale, thoughtful face.
- Neat dark 1840s clothing.
- Inside: seated with newspaper and cigar.
- Outside: dark overcoat, hat, cane, later a handkerchief at his mouth.
- Never visually confused with the old man.

### Old Man

- Sixty-five to seventy, short, very thin, apparently feeble.
- Deeply lined, contradictory expression: intelligence, caution, malice, terror, triumph, and despair without a literal supernatural transformation.
- Filthy ragged outer clothing and closely buttoned second-hand roquelaire.
- Fine but dirty linen visible beneath.
- Diamond and dagger appear only as a fleeting detail through a tear.

## World Locks

- London, autumn evening, circa 1840.
- Warm smoky coffeehouse interior behind curved glass.
- Dense street crowd, wet dark stone, humid fog, umbrellas, and flickering amber gas lamps.
- Cinematic historical graphic-novel realism with fine ink texture and restrained amber/blue-black color.
- No modern signs, electric lighting, cars, contemporary clothing, text captions, speech balloons, logos, or watermarks.

## Eight-Panel Sequence

1. Coffeehouse and bow window establish the observer against the crowd.
2. Anonymous faces flow past under gaslight.
3. The old man's face suddenly appears and breaks the pattern.
4. Contradictory emotions register in an extreme close-up.
5. The narrator reacts, fascinated and compelled.
6. He takes hat and cane and enters the crowd.
7. Over-the-shoulder pursuit reveals ragged clothing and fine linen.
8. A detail glimpse of diamond and dagger ends the sequence.

## Image-Generation Prompt

Use case: illustration-story

Asset type: project benchmark, single portrait page containing eight clearly separated cinematic storyboard panels.

Primary request: Create an eight-panel visual adaptation of Edgar Allan Poe's public-domain story “The Man of the Crowd,” covering the approved sequence above. Keep both recurring characters consistent in every panel.

Style/medium: premium cinematic historical graphic novel realism; fine ink texture; restrained color; anatomically natural faces; 1840 London accuracy.

Composition: portrait page, two columns by four rows, clean equal gutters, no captions or text. Vary shots exactly as described in the panel sequence.

Lighting/mood: smoky amber gaslight against blue-black rain and fog; psychologically tense, mysterious, grounded rather than supernatural.

Constraints: preserve the character and world locks; show the diamond and dagger only in panel eight; no text, speech balloons, modern objects, logos, or watermark.
